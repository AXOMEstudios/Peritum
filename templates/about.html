About Peritum

Learn about how to use Peritum as social network or knowledge platform!

The following introduction is readable here as well.

You made it to Peritum - great!

Welcome to Peritum, „the expert for everything“! On Peritum you can find the best solutions to your problems, read interesting articles and recipes and you can contribute by yourself as well in almost no time!


What is Peritum?

The idea behind Peritum is that, as we all know, everyone is an expert at something; someone might know thousands of cool things about miniature trains, other ones are experts in how to bake glittering cupcakes. When all of this knowledge, ideas and tips are coming to one point, this single point will get the expert of everything over time - Peritum.


Why „Peritum“?

That‘s a latin word meaning „Expert“.


Using Peritum

On the main page, you can find the hottest articles on the platform, a search bar for finding cool things, a footer with many useful shortcuts (which is on every site on the platform at the very bottom) and a navigation bar on the very top. This page should be the place to go when you want to find information.


Logging in to the platform

We‘re using AXOME auth for authentication. By logging in to the platform, you agree to AXOME‘s policies. All you need to get started is to click on one of the „Sign in“ buttons (either on the navigation bar or on the article reading page) and to log in with your AXOME account. If you do not have one, you can create an account for free here.


Writing an article

Click on the „+“ button in the navigation bar. Then enter a title and description for your article and click on the image to upload your own one. You can pick images from socials, your computer, stock websites and other sources. Make sure you do not violate the AXOME terms of service. Now you can start writing your article just like you would with a normal text editor. On the right side you can find helpful tips for a better experience of your readers. Hit the „Publish“ button to publish your article to Peritum. You can even take breaks of writing, your article is being saved on your local device.


Customizing your profile

To change how you appear on the platform to other people, open the settings by either hovering over your user in the navigation bar and choosing „Settings“ or clicking on the shortcut inside the footer on the very bottom of every page. You can change your biography (account description), profile picture and switch between users pr log out.


Outro

We hope this article could help you understanding Peritum! If you have any further questions, please do not hesitate contacting us! Support links are in the footer.
